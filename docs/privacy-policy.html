Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 20/11/2025

This privacy statement (“Privacy Policy”) explains how “Escapy” (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects and uses information.

We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

Personal Information

Escapy uses Firebase services to authenticate users. As part of this process, Firebase stores your email address and a unique user identifier (UID). These elements are required for secure access to your account and to link your games, history, and preferences.

We do not store additional personal information beyond what is required for account authentication and normal app operation.

Game and App Data

To enable gameplay, we store game-related data such as created games, game state, messages, clues, timers, and statistics. This data is only used inside the application and is not shared externally.

Non-Personal Information

We may collect anonymized technical data such as device type, operating system version, crash logs, and diagnostic information. These help us improve app stability, fix bugs, and understand app usage patterns.

Third-party services such as Google Crashlytics or Firebase Analytics may process this anonymized data on our behalf.

Account Deletion

You can request the complete deletion of your account. When your account is deleted, all associated personal data and game-related information will be permanently removed from our systems. This action is irreversible.

Children’s Privacy

Escapy does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age required by local data protection laws for independent online consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has created an account without your approval, you may contact us to request removal of the account.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. You are encouraged to review this page for any modifications. Changes become effective when posted here.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to request account deletion, please contact us.
